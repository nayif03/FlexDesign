THE JUMBOTRON

The Sony JumboTron made its debut at World's Fair 1985. The "Super Sign" on ABC's Times Square Studios facility was a very large Sony JumboTron. This unit was later replaced with a Mitsubishi Electric LED display. A jumbotron, sometimes referred to as Jumbovision, is a video display using large-screen television technology.
